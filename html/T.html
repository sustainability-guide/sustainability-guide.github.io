Travelling

Just like the busy bee of Manchester, travelling plays a constant role in our lives.
Here are the links to the different websites that will encourage you to travel sustainably.

  • Eco Passenger

    • Eco passenger is a tool that can be used to compare the energy consumption and CO2 emissions for different modes of transportation.

  • Walkit.com

    • Walkit.com is an urban walking route planner that provides you with the optimal walking route and inspirational ideas of how to make walking interesting.

  • Transport for Greater Manchester

    • The Transport for Greater Manchester has an excellent route planner with a comprehensive list of the different modes of transportation with the option to only select the desired service operator.

  • We are Cycling UK

    • We are Cycling UK is a unique organisation that features a journey planner for cycling, links to different cycling groups and clubs in your local area, and inspirational ideas to encourage people of all ages to cycle.

  • BlaBlaCar

    • BlaBlaCar is an online marketplace for carpooling. The website and mobile app connect drivers and passengers willing to travel together between cities and share the cost of the journey.
