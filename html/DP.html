Ways to make your home sustainable

    Turn off lights when you leave a room
    Use reuseable containers for snacks, water and other drinks
    Turn off appliances at the wall when they are not in use
    Select appliances with high energy-efficiency ratings.
    Swap regular light bulbs for eco bulbs.
    Use recycle bins whenever possible
    Install Smart Metering around your home
    Create a home vegetable garden
    Bike, Walk, Cycle or run everywhere

How to be sustainable at work

    Copying and printing on both sides of every sheet cuts your papers bills in half
    Recycle all paper waste and only print if absolutely necessary
    Utilise the cycle to work scheme that maybe available through your employee benefits
    Bike, Walk, Cycle or Run to work
    offer sustainability incentives at work
    Have recycle bins available on premises

How to be sustainable at school

    Educate staff and children on the benefits of sustainability
    Upgrade heating controls
    Set up a 'walking bus' or an alternative scheme
    Encourage parents to walk their children to school
    Create a school compost heap
    Create a classroom garden
